Welcome to the interactive poll {{p.title}} This poll is about deciding between a number of options. In the end, your group will pick exactly one option. Some options have already been suggested, but any participant in the poll can suggest further options at any time, until the poll closes. You can express your opinion about the suggested options by rating them. You can give each option a rating between 0 (don't approve) and 100 (approve for sure). If you are unsure about some option, you can also delegate that rating to someone you trust. In that case, that person's rating of the option counts for you as well. If you don't have the time for thinking about the option, you can also delegate all of your ratings to someone else. You can change your ratings or delegations at any time until the poll closes, which is on {{p.due}}. In fact, it often makes sense to follow how the poll results develop between now and then, and adjust your ratings to what you see, rate additional options that might get suggested, or add one yourself. That way, you can together find a good compromise or reach consensus.   If you want to know how the ratings are used by vodle to determine the results, see here.
