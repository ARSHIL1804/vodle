How to use vodle

How to vote

  • Select a poll from My polls or open a poll invitation that was sent to you.
     
  • Rate the options by using the sliders.
    A rating of, say, 80 means you would support this option if less then 80% oppose it (or, equivalently, if more than 20% support it). If your slider meets the light support bar to the right, then you support the option and the slider gets green.
     
  • It is usually a good idea to give all those options a rating larger than zero that you consider better than average, and to give your favourite option a rating of 100.
     
  • If you feel a good option is missing, just add it at the bottom of the list!
    Options are sorted by descending support, so your option will move upwards as it gains support. vodle works best with at least a handful of options.
     
  • The light bars to the right show current support, the pie charts to the left show current winning probabilities. See more information by pressing the dropdown buttons on the right.
